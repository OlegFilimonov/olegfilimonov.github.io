Three Min Passion

An app that allows users to explore a collection of interactive lessons. Each lesson is dynamically drawn and animated from server data and can be paused or rewound to each word. Each image or word can be a link to another lesson or quiz. A graph of all lessons allows users to explore and see what they have missed. Also contains a chat, credit system, coupon system, pure study mode and loop mode, and many more features.
